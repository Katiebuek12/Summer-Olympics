Archery is a sport that uses skill, patience and accuracy. In this sport, an archer uses a bow to propel arrows at a target. Archery started in the olypics games in 1900 and 84 nation have competed in archery events. To qualifiy to paricipate in the olympics for this sport you must be 16 or older, and you can either qualify by team or individual. In indiviual cometition, the archer begins with a ranking round and shoots 72 arrows. They are ranked by score and seeding in a single-elimination bracket. In team cometition, the team event uses the same results of the same ranking round as the indiviudal competition to determine seeding. The teams top 3 individual archers' scores are summed to get a team ranking round score and the top 4 teams recieve a bye into the quaterfinals. South Korea has the most number of medals, 23 gold, 9 silver and 7 bronze.
