Sailing has been scheduled for every Summer Olympic, but it was cancelled a few times due
to weather condition. Sailing employs the wind, acting on the sails, to move the ship on the
surface of the water. This sport was first played in the 18th century. The olympics uses three
different racing formats, a fleet race format, a match race format, and a mixed fleet/match race
format. Great Britain has the highest number of gold medals but the USA has the most
medals.
