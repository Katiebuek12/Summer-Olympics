Track and Field is a sport established on the skills of running, jumping and throwing. Track is
one of the oldest sports and was played in the Ancient Olympic Games. It has a prominent
position at the Summer Olympics since its inception in 1896 and is usually held in the main
stadium. There are many different events in track and each emphasizes different skills. In the
running category there are sprints (100 meters, 200 meters and 400 meter), middle distance
(800 meters, 1500 meters and mile run), long distance (3000 meter, 5000 meters and 10,000
meters), relay races and hurdling. In the category of jumping the different events are long
jump, triple jump, high jump and pole vault. And in throwing the events are shot put, discus
throw, javelin throw, and hammer throw. Track is a very competitive sport and has been in the
Olympics since its start.
